Who We Are

Hitaishi Constructions is a trusted name in the construction industry, dedicated to supplying high-quality building materials to contractors, builders, and homeowners. With a strong commitment to excellence, we aim to support every project with reliable products and exceptional service. Established with a vision to revolutionize the construction supply chain, we have grown into a leading provider known for our integrity and customer satisfaction.

Our team works closely with clients to understand their needs and provide tailored solutions, ensuring that every project is completed on time and within budget. At Hitaishi Constructions, we believe in building not just structures, but lasting relationships with our customers.

Our History

Our Beginnings

Hitaishi Constructions was founded in 2005 by a group of construction professionals who saw a gap in the market for reliable and affordable building materials. Starting as a small supplier in a local community, we quickly gained a reputation for our quality products and exceptional customer service.

Our Growth

In 2010, we introduced a wider range of products, including specialized materials for home interiors and plumbing. By 2015, we had established partnerships with leading manufacturers, ensuring a steady supply of top-notch materials.

Today

Today, Hitaishi Constructions stands as a symbol of trust and quality in the construction industry, with a legacy of supporting countless successful projects across the region.
